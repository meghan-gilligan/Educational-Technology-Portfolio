01 — About

Designing for the Person

My work has always been about meeting people in the middle of change. For more than fifteen years, I have worked at the intersection of education, employment, and human development. I believe good learning design begins with deep respect for the learner.

I began my career as an employment counsellor and eventually built a practice spanning youth programming and career coaching.

My specialization in adult learning with technology has given me the theoretical grounding for my practice. I use frameworks like backward design and ground assessment in clearly defined outcomes[cite: 36, 43].

I am drawn to roles at OPG where I can contribute to programs that take equity and accessibility seriously[cite: 35, 54].
